1. Drawing Tab

  • Click Pen and select your appropriate thickness and colour.
  • Draw on the canvas with your finger or stylus pen.
  • To erase, click eraser icon and drag over the portion of the drawing that you want to erase. You can control the eraser thickness by the same size control box of pen.
  • You can use Undo-redo by clicking once.
  • To add page, click add page icon next after redo button.
  • To delete page, select the page and click the delete page icon next to the add page.
  • To move and cut stokes, click on the select icon next to the zoom out button and then select your stokes that you want to move or cut by selecting it through rectangular selecting box. After that, move your stokes as you want and to cut, click the cut button after selecting stokes.
  • To hide left sidebar, click the first button in Drawing Tab.

2. Insert Tab

  • To add image/file, go to Insert tab and click on the first button (Image icon) and go to your browser and select the image/file and click select. The selected image/file will upload on the canvas. To move and resize, drag the corner handle and move/resize the image/file.
  • To add shapes, go to the insert tab and click shape icon which will open a shape panel from which you can select your required shape. After selecting the specific shape, drag over the canvas with your appropriate direction and orientation.
  • To delete a shape, select the shape first and then click delete button next after the shapes button in insert tab.

3. Slideshow Tab

  • To work in slideshow/preview mode, go to Slideshow tab and click the start icon and then click on the back button on your device and then you will see Prev, Next, Exit buttons. To exit from slideshow, click Exit button.

4. Video Tab

  • To record your work as a video with audio, go to Video Tab and click the first button and then click the next button successively. It will give you a video which is basically a trial. Delete it from browser and do the same process again which will give you a proper video with audio as recording.

5. Export Tab

  • To Export your work as PNG, PDF, and PPT,JSON, go to Export Tab and select your appropriate choice of export. After exporting, it will store in your browser.
    • How to get Your Exported whiteboard:?
  • Go to your browser where you have opened the whiteboard and click three dots and go to Downloads.
  • To work in cloud, click save to cloud which will store everything in cloud and it will load auto when you open it next. To share your whiteboard with content, click share which will give you a sharable link of a specific board with its id and you can share it with others.Once you share they can see all changes you will do in your that board, you need not share multiple times.
