User Guide

1
Drawing Tab
  • Click Pen and select your appropriate thickness and colour.
  • Draw on the canvas with your finger or stylus pen.
  • To erase, click the eraser icon and drag over the portion you want to erase. Control thickness with the same size box.
  • Use Undo / Redo by clicking once.
  • To add a page, click the Add Page icon after the redo button.
  • To delete a page, select it and click the Delete Page icon.
  • To move/cut strokes, use the Select icon, draw a rectangular selection, then drag or cut.
  • To hide the sidebar, click the first button in Drawing Tab.
  • To scroll the canvas, click Scroll Mode and scroll up/down/left/right.
2
Insert Tab
  • To add an image/file, click the Image icon in Insert tab, browse to your file, and click Select. Drag corner handles to resize.
  • To add shapes, click the Shape icon to open the shape panel, select a shape, then drag on the canvas.
  • To delete a shape, select it first then click the Delete button.
3
Slideshow Tab
  • Go to Slideshow Tab and click the start icon. Press Back on your device to reveal Prev / Next / Exit buttons.
  • Click Exit to leave slideshow mode.
4
Video Tab
  • Go to Video Tab and click the first button, then the next button. The first recording is a trial — delete it and repeat for a proper recording with audio.
  • Use Pause / Resume buttons to control recording. Click the last button to open the Recordings panel and download via Restore.
5
Export Tab
  • Export your work as PNG, PDF, or PPT from the Export Tab. Files are saved to your browser downloads.
  • To retrieve exports: Click the three-dot menu in your browser → Downloads.
  • Click Save Cloud to sync to cloud storage — auto-loaded on next open.
  • Click Storage to view the board JSON and storage usage.
